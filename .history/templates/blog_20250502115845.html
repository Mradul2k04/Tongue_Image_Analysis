About the Project - Tongue Health Analyzer

This web application uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to examine tongue images and provide health recommendations.

The color and texture of the tongue can be strong indicators of health conditions. The app accepts up to three tongue images (front, left, and right) and uses a trained CNN to assess possible health indicators.
